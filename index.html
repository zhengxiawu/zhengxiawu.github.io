Biography

郑侠武，厦门大学人工智能研究院副教授、博士生导师，鹏城国家实验室双聘副研究员。长期从事自动机器学习（AutoML）、神经网络结构搜索（NAS）与模型压缩研究，围绕“全流程无偏空间设计、信息几何黑盒优化、动态分布保序估计”形成系统性成果。研究方向包括 AutoML、NAS、模型压缩、大模型高效部署，以及多模态与智能体系统中的高效推理。

As of the 2026 academic CV and Google Scholar snapshots, he has published 130+ papers, including many papers in CCF-A venues, TPAMI, IJCV, CVPR, ICCV, NeurIPS, ICLR, and ICML. His work has been adopted in national and IEEE standards for neural-network representation and model compression, and has been translated into industrial systems with partners including Huawei, HONOR, VIVO, Tencent, and others.
